About Me

Background

My name is Gage Nesbit and I am currently an undergraduate student at Montana State University. I will be graduating in December of 2024 with my Bachelors of Science in computer science. After graduating I plan on moving back home to western Washington state to pursue my career in software engineering. I am passionate about software and would like to apply the skills that I have learned while earning my degree.

My Approach to Software

Developing software is all about creating solutions and tools for problems that people are having. This process may start with a simple idea but through a continuous process of refinement, I can apply my creativity and problem-solving skills. I strive to create software that functions well, is maintainable, and follows sound design principles.
